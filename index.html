Disney Adventures

JAN 2026 - Present

The next chapter begins! Moving to Florida and starting the Disney College Program.

Jan 14, 2026

Now that I had my tickets, the plan was to get to Disney World right as they open but I ended up doing homework until 3am and didn't arrive until around 10am. Either way, it was my first time going to Disney World so I went to Magic Kingdom. There is too much footage and images to have a description of them all so here are the pictures all grouped together. It was real cheesy but I had a hoot, may or may not have even had some tears during the Fireworks (wasn't expecting that but I swear there was dust in the air).

Jan 13, 2026

After getting my free 3 Day ParkHopper Passes for Disney World all sorted out, I met a new friend in line who joined me at the Welcome To Florida Event Party going on. They had a giant life size gator (totally not an inflatable slide), free pizza, stickers, and a photo spot.

Jan 13, 2026

This guy at the party had a parrot hat.

Jan 13, 2026

You would be crazy to think that I didn't go on the giant gator slide.

Jan 13, 2026

There was also a character meet & greet, but you can only pick one group of characters to take a picture with.

Jan 12, 2026

Once everything was finished for the day, I walked about 10 minutes to the nearest Target to get myself some survival food. Nothing like Oatmeal, Bananas, Bread, and Peanut Butter for $13.

Jan 12, 2026

I have 3 roommates in my Dorm who are all very nice and were sharing all kinds of tips on how to get around here. They are from Peru, Canada, and Miami. My room has a bed, cabinet, closet, safety box (I brought a lock), and my own bathroom/shower. It's very nice and I was easily able to unpack everything.

Jan 12, 2026

After probably 1.5 hours of waiting, getting my information checked, being fingerprinted, taking my ID Picture, and receiving my Entry Pass... I was finally able to get my Dorm Room where I'll be staying.

Jan 12, 2026

About 5 minutes before I was about to Check Out of the Hotel Room, my friend calls me saying he lost his wallet. I found it in the room but had to drop off my luggage first or I'll be late. I ended up Ubering to give my friend his wallet, Ubering back to drop off my luggage, then Ubering right back a few minutes after to start my Check In. Luckily there were alot of people arriving for the program today, so being late was fine (my friend also paid me back for those Uber rides).

Jan 12, 2026

My friend had his Check In time at 8am whereas I had mine at 10.20am, so he Ubered to the Flamingo Crossing Village (which is where we'll be staying), while I stayed to clean up the Hotel Room and get ready.

Jan 12, 2026

I made sure to eat whatever I could at the Hotel Breakfast as I wasn't sure when my next meal may be. Then I ended up having to explode the toilet a few times right after.

Jan 11, 2026

My friend from school was also doing the Disney Program and arrived earlier that day after a 40 hour Amtrak Train ride. I made sure to have booked this room early at the TownePlace Suites Hotel for the night. Then we celebrated with some pizza.

Jan 11, 2026

After almost 3 hours of flying, I have made it to the Orlando Airport. It was strange to be in 80 degree weather after just leaving Chicago where it was 30 degrees only a few hours before.

Jan 11, 2026

I almost missed the flight too as I forgot I had to still Check In to get my seat and ended up waiting until the sign read "Boarding Complete", luckily the workers there were able to get me the last seat as the entire plane was completely full.

Jan 11, 2026

Apparently my "Free Carry On" bag was too large for O'Hare's Dimensions so I had to pay an extra $45 to make it a Checked Bag. Then I waited at Terminal K9 for my flight.

Jan 11, 2026

I had everything needed to spend 7 months in Florida and was packing my luggage until 4am.

Jan 11, 2026

Went to make one last Amazon Return at Kohls before heading to O'Hare Airport.
