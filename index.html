We spent a day at Animal Kingdom! We went on a safari seeing giraffes, elephants, rhinos, and lions up close, then traveled back in time on the DINOSAUR ride. After grabbing some food, we headed to Hollywood Studios to see the giant guitar at Rock 'n' Roller Coaster, explore Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge (featuring the Millennium Falcon and Stormtroopers), and survive the drop on the Tower of Terror
